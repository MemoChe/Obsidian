<mark style="background: #FF5582A6;">1.- ENERGIZING QUINOA AND FRUIT SMOOTHIE</mark>
Ingredients:
1/2 cup cooked quinoa
1 banana
1/2 cup blueberries
1 cup fresh spinach
1 cup almond milk (or any vegetable milk)
1 tablespoon chia seeds
Honey or agave syrup to taste (optional)
Preparation
Place all ingredients in a blender.
Blend until smooth and homogeneous.
Serve immediately and enjoy.
<mark style="background: #FF5582A6;">2.- OATMEAL WITH FRUITS AND NUTS</mark>
Ingredients:
1 cup of oatmeal
2 cups almond milk (or any milk of your choice)
1/2 cup strawberries, sliced
1/2 cup blueberries
1 banana, sliced
1/4 cup chopped walnuts
1 tablespoon flaxseed
Honey or agave syrup to taste (optional)
Preparation
Cook the oatmeal with the milk over medium heat until smooth and creamy.
Serve the oatmeal in a bowl and add the fruits, nuts and flaxseeds.
Sweeten with honey or agave syrup if desired.
<mark style="background: #FF5582A6;">3.- WHOLE WHEAT TOAST WITH AVOCADO AND EGGS</mark>
Ingredients:
2 slices of whole wheat bread
1 ripe avocado
2 eggs
Juice of 1/2 lemon
Salt and pepper to taste
Sesame seeds (optional)
Preparation
Toast the slices of whole wheat bread.
Mash the avocado in a bowl and add the lemon juice, salt and pepper to taste.
Cook the eggs to taste (poached, scrambled or fried).
Spread the avocado on the toast and place the eggs on top.
Sprinkle with sesame seeds if desired.
<mark style="background: #FF5582A6;">4.- YOGURT AND FRUIT PARFAIT</mark>
Ingredients:
1 cup plain yogurt
1/2 cup unsweetened granola
1/2 cup strawberries, sliced
1/2 cup blueberries
1 banana, sliced
Honey to taste (optional)
Preparation
In a glass or bowl, place a layer of yogurt.
Add a layer of granola followed by a layer of fruit.
Repeat the layers until the glass or bowl is full.
Sweeten with honey if desired.
<mark style="background: #FF5582A6;">5.- SCRAMBLED EGGS WITH SPINACH AND TOMATOES</mark>
Ingredients:
3 eggs
1 cup fresh spinach
1 tomato, diced
1/4 onion, finely chopped
1 clove garlic, minced
Salt and pepper to taste
Olive oil
Preparation
Heat a little olive oil in a skillet over medium heat.
Add the onion and garlic and sauté until golden brown.
Add the tomato and spinach, and cook until the spinach has wilted.
Beat the eggs in a bowl, season with salt and pepper, and add them to the pan.
Cook the eggs, stirring constantly, until cooked through.
Serve hot.
<mark style="background: #FF5582A6;">6.- OATMEAL AND  BANANA PANCAKES</mark>
Ingredients:
1 ripe banana
1 cup oatmeal
1 cup almond milk (or any milk of your choice)
1 egg
1 teaspoon baking powder
1 teaspoon vanilla extract
Honey or agave syrup to serve
Fresh fruit to serve
Preparation
In a bowl, mash the banana.
Add the oats, milk, egg, baking powder and vanilla extract and mix well.
Heat a skillet over medium heat and grease lightly.
Pour small amounts of the mixture into the pan, forming pancakes.
Cook until bubbles appear on the surface, then flip and cook on the other side until golden brown.
Serve with honey or agave syrup and fresh fruit.
